About Match-a-Matcha

Match-a-Matcha is a personalized recipe curator for matcha and coffee lovers. Instead of scrolling endless recipe lists, you answer three quick questions and we suggest a drink + pastry pairing that fits your vibe.

Whether you're a student cramming for midterms, a creative looking for cozy cafe vibes at home, or a busy professional who wants something simple but special, we've got you.

  • Curated drink recipes with clear steps
  • Suggested pastry pairings for each drink
  • Seasonal and classic drink collections
